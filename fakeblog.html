William Fritts

I am currently a medic in the Army National Guard. I joined in 2013 after some major life events. I always wanted to be in the Army as a child, but I put it on hold when I first graduated high school because I wanted to go to school to get a degree first. I attained a bachelors degree in History. Shortly after I went to work full time, deciding to delay entry into the Military. After some time in the workforce, I decided it was past time to join. I enlisted.

I went to basic at Ft. Benning. Followed by AIT in Ft. Sam Houston. I came back to Washington and have happily continued to serve. I decided shortly after coming back that I wanted to continue my education. First I explored continuing down the path of medicine. However, I quickly found out that was not for me. I then gravitated towards science, and eventually found my way back to coding. I had done some inital coding in high school and found that I liked it, but I didnt want to make a career of it at the time. However, things change, and here I am.
